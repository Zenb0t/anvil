# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Giving and accepting feedback in good faith
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Public or private intimidation
- Publishing others' private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards of acceptable behavior.

To report conduct concerns, contact the maintainers through repository channels. Reports will be reviewed and handled fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1.
